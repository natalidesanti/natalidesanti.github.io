Paper highlights:
- Trains a conditional normalizing flow on CAMELS hydrodynamical simulations to model the joint probability of galaxy and halo properties conditioned on cosmological and astrophysical parameters.
- Enables efficient marginalisation over nuisance parameters and serves as a generative model for halo masses, galaxy properties, and scaling relations.
- Provides a flexible probabilistic framework for modeling the galaxy–halo connection across a wide parameter space of cosmological and astrophysical parameters.
