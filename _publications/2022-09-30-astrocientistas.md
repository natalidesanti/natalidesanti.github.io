Paper highlights:
- Proposes a convolutional neural network pipeline to denoise cosmological covariance matrices estimated from limited data.
- Shows that noisy covariances built from few power spectra can be significantly improved, approaching those obtained from much larger simulation sets.
- Demonstrates that the denoised covariances enable more accurate recovery of cosmological parameters in MCMC analyses.
